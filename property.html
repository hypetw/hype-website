03 — FAQ

Questions, answered.

If yours isn't here, email property@hypedigital.ie and we'll reply the same day.

Do I need a professional camera?

No. Any smartphone from the last four years will do. We optimise the images for you — exposure, colour, sharpness, perspective. Just walk the rooms and shoot wide.

Is the output real or AI-generated?

The rooms and the property are 100% real — your own photos. We use AI to enhance lighting, straighten walls, replace dull skies, and optionally stage empty rooms. We never fabricate rooms that don't exist.

How long does delivery actually take?

Standard turnaround is one working day (under 24 hours). Rush orders are available for 6-hour delivery at an additional cost.

What formats do I get?

Every listing is delivered as high-res JPEG plus portal-sized exports for Daft, MyHome, Rightmove, and social. Custom export presets for your agency portal available on request.

Can I stage an empty property?

Yes. Our virtual staging add-on furnishes empty rooms in a chosen style — Scandinavian, Modern Irish, Contemporary Luxury, and more.

Is this compliant with property advertising guidelines?

Yes. We enhance only lighting, colour, and staging — never misrepresent size, condition, or features. Virtually staged images are clearly labelled on delivery.
