Process

Brief. Production. Delivery.

Three phases. One team across all of them. Most projects move from first conversation to delivered assets in five to fourteen days.

Phase 01

Brief

We start with the property and the audience. What kind of buyer this scheme is for, what the architecture is saying, what role the imagery has to play across the launch.

Phase 02

Production

One studio team produces every asset in-house. Single creative direction across image, motion, and print. No outsourced freelancers and no compromise in the handover.

Phase 03

Delivery

Assets delivered ready for market in the formats your channels actually use. Most engagements close inside five to fourteen days from first brief.

Questions

What partners ask first.

01

What do you need from us to start?

The photography the listing already has. You send the existing images of the property and we produce everything from there: the film, the graded stills, the social cuts, the collateral, all delivered ready for market and yours to run across the launch. There is nothing to arrange on your side beyond sending the files.

02

Do we need a new photo shoot or a crew on site?

No. We work entirely from imagery that already exists, so there is no shoot to book, no crew to travel, and nothing to schedule around weather or access. That is the whole point of the studio. The work that used to need a production budget and a clear week in the calendar now starts from the photos already sitting in your listing.

03

What do we actually receive?

A complete set, not a single file. The property film, the editorial listing imagery, the cuts your channels use, and print-ready collateral, all carrying one creative direction so every piece looks like it belongs to the same campaign. You get what the launch needs, in the formats your channels actually use.

04

How long does it take?

Listing imagery is delivered in 48 hours as standard. A full engagement, the film and the complete asset set, typically moves from first brief to finished work in five to fourteen days. No shoot in the schedule is the reason it moves that fast.

05

What if the home is not built yet, or our photos are basic?

Both are fine. For schemes still off-plan we produce finished-home visuals buyers can picture living in. For existing homes we work with whatever you have, including straightforward phone photography. The starting point matters far less than most people assume.

06

Can you keep it consistent across a whole scheme?

Yes, and that is where it matters most. One team holds a single creative direction across every asset and every unit, so a multi-home launch reads as one premium brand rather than a set of listings shot by different hands on different days.

07

How much does it cost?

Engagements start from €1,500 a month, with schemes quoted to scope, since a multi-unit launch is a different brief from a single premium listing. Set against the cost and the delay of arranging a fresh shoot for every property, it is the more efficient way to bring a portfolio to market.

08

Do you only work in Ireland?

No. The studio is based in Cork and works across Ireland, the UK, and internationally. Because everything starts from imagery that already exists, the location of the property places no limit on what we can deliver. Our flagship film, a beachfront estate at Lyford Cay, was produced entirely from the listing's existing photography.

If the studio looks like the right partner for your scheme, send a brief. Every inquiry is reviewed personally, with a reply inside two working days.
